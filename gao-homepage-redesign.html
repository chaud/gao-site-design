Holding Government Accountable

GAO provides fact-based, nonpartisan information to Congress. Often called the "congressional watchdog," we investigate how the federal government spends taxpayer dollars and help improve government operations.

About the Comptroller General

Gene L. Dodaro
8th Comptroller General of the United States

As Comptroller General, Gene Dodaro leads GAO and is the government's chief accountability officer. He has led GAO since 2010, driving improvements in federal programs and operations while delivering significant financial benefits to taxpayers.
